About Me
Thanks for your interest, here is a quick story of me and this website.
With a solid foundation in both IT Audit and Data Science, I am passionate about leveraging the power of data to drive insights and enhance the integrity of audit processes. My journey began with an enriching internship in the IT Audit field, where I developed a keen understanding of auditing principles and methodologies. By employing advanced analytical techniques, I aim to enhance the accuracy, efficiency, and fairness of audit processes, providing more robust and insightful outcomes.
I am always eager to connect with like-minded professionals and explore opportunities to apply my skills in innovative and impactful ways. Let's connect and discuss how we can leverage data science to revolutionise audit practices!
